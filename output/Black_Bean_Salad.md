# Black Bean Salad

![Recipe image](https://food.fnr.sndimg.com/content/dam/images/food/fullset/2023/4/04/FN_Guy-Fieri_Black-Bean-Salad_s4x3.jpg.rend.hgtvcom.1280.1280.suffix/1680711014916.webp)

- **Source:** foodnetwork.co.uk
- **URL:** https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/guy-fieri/black-bean-salad-recipe-1946862
- **Yields:** 8 servings
- **Total:** 70 min
- **Prep:** 10 min

## Ingredients

- 2 cans black beans, drained, about 30 ounces
- 1 (15-ounce) can corn, drained
- 2 Roma tomatoes, diced
- 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
- 1/4 cup diced red onion
- 1/4 cup diced green onions
- 1/4 cup diced pineapple
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro leaves
- 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
- 4 tablespoons sherry vinegar
- Juice of 1/2 lime
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- Pinch ground cumin

## Steps

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour.
